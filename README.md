## рџ“Љ Key Business Insights

### 1. Baseline Churn

The overall customer churn rate is **20.37%**, indicating a significant customer retention challenge.

### 2. Geographic Risk

Germany represents the highest-risk region. Despite having high average balances, it experiences the highest churn levels.

### 3. Demographic Risk

Customers aged **50+** are more likely to leave the bank, making them a high-risk segment.

### 4. Customer Engagement Matters

Inactive customers are significantly more likely to churn compared to active customers.

### 5. Weak Predictors

Credit Score and Estimated Salary alone do not strongly explain customer churn.
